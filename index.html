Keeping exclusivity in mind

Design Benefits

8 Acres Of Open Space

Klassik Landmark is spread over 11 acres. Unlike most other developments around Sarjapur Road. We have designed a traffic free, pedestrian friendly podium level.

Aerodynamic Design

The 'wing-shaped' design of the towers, maximises airflow around each building. Unlike a rectangular shaped building, every balcony and window in Klassik Landmark receives ample light and fresh air.

Only 3 Flats Per Floor

Every lift lobby only serves 3 apartments per floor. Each lift lobby has 2 lifts, one 13 person lift and one 6 person lift.

Optimised Ventilation

The ventilation in the flats is a direct benefit of the 'wing-shaped' towers. Unlike most other flats in Bangalore, we have discretely placed all toilets on the outer edge of the building, to ensure ample sunlight and ventilation.

25,000sft Clubhouse

It is big as well. The 25,000sft clubhouse is well equipped to service the 700 apartments in Klassik Landmark.

Olympic Size Pool

It is big. You can swim an olympic length lap from one end to the other. The swimming pool is a crowning feature of the project. Some of Klassik Landmark's best views are from flats overlooking the pool.
